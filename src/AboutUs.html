About NBA Nation

NBA Nation is a website dedicated to address the information needs of the NBA fans and community. We provide a access to news about NBA from different sources. We also give various info about your favourite teams in the NBA such as game results and standings. Players are also not forgotten as their accomplishments in the season is also given coverage at NBA Nation.

The future of NBA Nation

As an aspiring and growning team, we, at NBA nation, try to further expand NBA Nation. In the future we expect our website streaming live games for a reasonable price and serving exclusive trade news to you, the fans. Furthermore, we want, in the future, to build our own community which will discuss basketball in NBA and share interesting informations each other.
